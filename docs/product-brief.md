# Product Brief: Realtime Support Inbox

**Problem**
Support agents need a reliable realtime inbox to triage conversations, respond quickly, and track status changes.

**Audience**
Internal support agents at a SaaS company.

**Core Features**
- Conversation list with status, priority, and assignment.
- Message history with pagination.
- Realtime updates for new messages, status changes, and presence.
- Safe reconnect and recovery behavior.

**Success Criteria**
- Agents can handle conversations without refreshing the app.
- Realtime updates appear quickly and consistently.
- The UI handles offline or reconnecting states gracefully.
